About me

My name is Lidіia, I am 23 years old and my role in this life is to give people memories.

Let’s start with the fact that my photography is, first of all, sincere emotions, laughter and smiles, facial wrinkles and your uniqueness. I love the movie atmosphere, retro and black and white colors. For me, photography is not about standard photos with perfect posing, it is the meaning during some of my tasks to feel you, to open your soul and the depth of emotions in order to save them on film, which in the future will warm its warmth from pleasant memories. During a session with me, we walk a lot, talk, laugh, listen to music and have fun!

Your Liddy.
